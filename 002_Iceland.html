Iceland Trip 2018

A New Frontier

Wanting to continue the adventure, 2 friends and I flew to Iceland and rented a camper van for a week of exploring the untamed wilderness of Iceland.  This trip was full of near disasters and some of the most beautiful and unique landscapes on the earth. Some notable spots are.

  • The Westfjords
  • The Valley of Thor (Þórsmörk)
  • Black Sand Beach Puffins
